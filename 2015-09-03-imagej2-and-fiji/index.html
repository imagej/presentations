A colleague told me once:

It's not the code that matters—it's the community.

But you can't build a house with your bare hands.

Well, maybe you can...

...but there are limits.

You can't build a house alone, either.

Both tools, and people to create
and use them, are necessary.

But wait...

  • Creating software is not like building a house!
  • It's more like designing a house.
  • Once you have the design, you can mass produce it.
  • But the design keeps evolving...
  • Is this analogy even helpful anymore?

No more analogies!

ImageJ2's Mission

  1. Lead ImageJ development with a clear vision
  2. Create the next ImageJ, based on community needs
  3. Collaborate with others whenever beneficial
  4. Make ImageJ useful to a broad community
  5. Maintain backwards compatibility with ImageJ 1.x
  6. Provide a central online resource for ImageJ
