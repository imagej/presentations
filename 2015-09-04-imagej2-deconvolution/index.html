Why deconvolve?

  • Improve measurements
  • Improve science
  • Imaging devices are not perfect
  • Images are blurred and noisy
  • Deconvolution is a computational technique that:
    • reduces blur
    • enhances contrast
    • reduces noise
  • If used properly deconvolution
    • improves measurements
    • (possibly) improves science
